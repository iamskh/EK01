About Eberkow

To be honest we haven't been around the block a lot, we are young enthusiastic startup with big ideas and immense amount of energy devoted to our vision. We already have experienced failures and success in other startups and all those journeys have let us to working on our next sustainable journey this time aimed at market in Africa.

We are great advocates of the startup community globally but we are more big on startup tech in Africa and creating new opportunities in the region.The products we are building are mainly aimed at Africa markets with the intention of building strong nurturing partnerships with companies from east and west markets. Our Products provide parallel real time informations to SME’s based in Africa and companies based outside Africa that have interest in Africa markets.
